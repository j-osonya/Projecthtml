What is cyber security?!
Hide
Cyber Security consists of a set of practices, technologies, and processes specifically designed to protect devices, networks and data from unauthorized, external access. It involves detecing, preventing, and responding to unauthorised access or attacks oncomputer systems, data, and networks, with the objective of maintaining integrity, confidentiality and availability of data.
